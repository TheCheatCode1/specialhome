We Offer Different Services To Improve Your Health

We at Specialized Home Health Services, Inc are committed to provide the highest quality health care possible at the convenience and comfort of your home. After all, there is no place like home.

Our Home Health Care services are designed to provide comprehensive, high-quality care in the comfort of your home. Our skilled healthcare professionals work closely with patients and their families to develop personalized care plans that meet individual needs. We offer a range of services, including medical care, therapy, and assistance with daily activities, ensuring our patients receive the best possible care without leaving their homes. Assisting The patient with personal care and hygiene, light housekeeping, meal preparation, and other related activities, directed and supervised by a Registered Nurse.

Our Skilled Nursing services offer specialized medical care for patients who require more intensive, round-the-clock monitoring and treatment. Our experienced nurses are equipped to handle complex medical conditions, administer medications, and perform various medical procedures. We strive to provide high-quality, patient-centered care, ensuring the best outcomes for our patients. We provide prescribed physical therapy treatment, aimed to developing and restore functions of the muscles and joints; assisting the patient in improving and facilitating maximum self-care; to returning to optimum function
Individualized care, to promote, maintain, and assist patients in restoring health and minimizing the effects of their illness and disability.
Personalized teachings and education for the patient and the family, on preventive and rehabilitative nursing procedures

We provide a variety of Therapy Services, including physical, occupational, and speech therapy. Our licensed therapists work with patients to improve their mobility, strength, and communication skills, helping them regain independence and improve their overall quality of life. Each therapy plan is tailored to meet the specific needs and goals of the patient.

Personal Care Assistance is designed to help patients with daily living activities such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and meal preparation. Our compassionate caregivers provide respectful and dignified assistance, ensuring that our patients can maintain their independence and comfort in their own homes.

We understand the importance of holistic care, which is why we offer Emotional and Spiritual Support services. Our team includes counselors and spiritual advisors who provide support and guidance to patients and their families, helping them cope with the emotional and spiritual challenges of illness and end-of-life care.
